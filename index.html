Online Dues Payment for Members

Brother Knights -

Again this year we are making this convenient online payment option available for the annual dues statements.

Thank you for your continued support through prayers, event participation and membership dues. Your dues and donations play a large part in helping us meet the many financial support needs in our parish and local community.

There has been no increase in membership dues.  Dues are $50 per regular member when paid on or after January 15, 2020. The specific amount due for each member is reflected in the annual dues statement. If you have not received your dues statement, please contact our Financial Secretary.

As in past years, you have the opportunity to participate in the Support Our Seminarians (SOS) fund drive and to support Gibault Children's Services. Recommended donations are $5.00 for each program.

Please complete your selections in the form below and click "Pay Now" to complete your transaction. A payment confirmation will be sent to the email you provide.
